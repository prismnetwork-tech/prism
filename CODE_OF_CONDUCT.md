# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

Project spaces must remain professional, safe and constructive. Participation
is open regardless of age, disability, ethnicity, gender identity or
expression, nationality, race, religion, sexual orientation, experience level
or socioeconomic status.

## Expected behavior

- Discuss ideas and evidence, not personal characteristics.
- Give specific, actionable technical feedback.
- Respect privacy and confidentiality.
- Disclose conflicts of interest.
- Accept correction and de-escalate disagreements.
- Prioritize system safety when changes affect funds, infrastructure or users.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, stalking, sexualized conduct, deliberate
disruption, doxxing and publication of private information are prohibited.
Security research must follow the coordinated-disclosure process.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to `conduct@prismnetwork.tech`. Maintainers
may remove content, restrict participation or permanently exclude participants
when necessary to protect the project and its community.

Reports are handled as confidentially as practical. Retaliation against a
reporter or witness is prohibited.
